Chiang Mai

Chiang Mai is a vibrant city located in mountainous northern Thailand. It has become a popular destination for digital nomads and developers due to its affordable cost of living, reliable internet connectivity, and thriving tech community. Many co-working spaces and cafes offer ideal environments for remote work.

In addition to its appeal to developers, Chiang Mai is also a haven for cyclists. The city and its surrounding countryside offer scenic routes and challenging trails for cyclists of all levels. Whether you prefer leisurely rides through the city's historic Old Town or adrenaline-pumping mountain biking adventures in the nearby hills, Chiang Mai has something for every cyclist.
